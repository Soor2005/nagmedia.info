Cold outreach is a marketing or sales strategy where a business or individual contacts potential customers who have not expressed prior interest in their products or services. This outreach is typically done through methods like cold calling, cold emailing, or social media messaging (we Use Only Cold Emailing).

The goal of cold outreach is to introduce the business or its offerings to new prospects, generate leads, and ultimately convert them into customers.

Cold outreach requires a targeted approach and often involves personalized messages to capture the recipient's attention and interest.
